## Primary Books

### 1. The Expectant Father
- **Author:** Armin A. Brott
- **Publisher:** Abbeville Press
- **ISBN:** 9780789214041
- **Edition:** 5th
- **Coverage:** Month-by-month guide, baby development, partner support, labor, postpartum
- **Legal Access:** https://www.abbeville.com/products/the-expectant-father-1
- **Also Listed:** https://www.accartbooks.com/uk/book/the-expectant-father-2/
- **Archive.org:** https://archive.org/details/expectantfatherf0000brot_r0m9

### 2. We're Pregnant! The First-Time Dad's Pregnancy Handbook
- **Author:** Adrian Kulp
- **Publisher:** Callisto Publishing
- **ISBN:** 9781939754684
- **Coverage:** Month-by-month for first-time dads, milestones, practical guidance
- **Legal Access:** https://bookshop.org/p/books/we-re-pregnant-the-first-time-dad-s-pregnancy-handbook-adrian-kulp/d5a94a9ac5ea8931
- **Barnes & Noble:** https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/were-pregnant-the-first-time-dads-pregnancy-handbook-adrian-kulp/1128179595
- **Alibris:** https://www.alibris.com/Were-Pregnant-the-First-Time-Dads-Pregnancy-Handbook-Adrian-Kulp/book/40031352

### 3. The Birth Partner
- **Author:** Penny Simkin
- **Publisher:** Harvard Common Press / Quarto
- **ISBN:** 9781558324923 (earlier); 9780760393239 (6th ed.)
- **Coverage:** Practical childbirth support manual, used by partners, doulas, birth educators
- **Legal Access:** https://clc.overdrive.com/media/1044821 (OverDrive)
- **6th Edition:** https://www.quarto.com/books/9780760393239/the-birth-partner-sixth-revised-edition
- **AbeBooks:** https://www.abebooks.com/9781558321953/Birth-Partner-Everything-Need-Know-1558321950/plp

### 4. Dad's Guide to Pregnancy For Dummies
- **Authors:** Matthew M.F. Miller, Sharon Perkins
- **Publisher:** Wiley
- **ISBN:** 9781119867159 (3rd edition)
- **Coverage:** Comprehensive A-Z pregnancy guide from a dad's perspective
- **Legal Access:** https://books.google.com/books/about/Dad_s_Guide_to_Pregnancy_For_Dummies.html?id=T8V6EAAAQBAJ
- **Wiley:** https://www.wiley.com/en-be/Dad's+Guide+To+Pregnancy+For+Dummies,+2nd+Edition-p-9781118860458
- **Archive.org:** https://archive.org/details/dadsguidetopregn0000mill_k4g9

### 5. The New Dad's Playbook
- **Author:** Benjamin Watson
- **Publisher:** Baker Books
- **Coverage:** Faith-based fatherhood preparation, practical and emotional guidance
- **Legal Access:** https://store.focusonthefamily.com/the-new-dads-playbook/

### 6. First-Time Dad Pregnancy Handbook
- **Author:** Watson R. Ward
- **Publisher:** Rockridge Press
- **Coverage:** Week-by-week expectant father guide
- **Listing:** https://www.barnesandnoble.com/w/first-time-dad-pregnancy-handbook-watson-r-ward/1145847332
