Section C

Financial Fortress

Build financial confidence from Week 4 forward. No stress — just clear steps.

🏥
Insurance Navigation
Call your provider: confirm maternity coverage, deductibles, OB and hospital network, NICU benefit, and pediatric coverage from birth.
👔
Paternity Leave Strategy
In NJ: check FMLA (federal), NJ FLI (paid family leave), and your employer's own policy. Start paperwork early — it takes longer than expected.
💰
Baby Fund Setup
Open a dedicated savings account for baby costs. Target: 3 months of projected baby expenses before due date. Start with $50/week now.

Relationship Compass

Nurture your partnership, communicate clearly, and establish solid family boundaries together.

🧭
Weekly Couple Check-Ins
Create space for empathy: ask how she's holding up, review the upcoming weekly agenda, and talk through any mutual anxieties without judgment.
🕯️
Intimacy Guidelines
Maintain connection through the physical and emotional shifts of each trimester. Understand when to prioritize gentle touch, comforting words, and validation.
⚖️
Division of Labor
Negotiate household responsibilities proactively. Step up and take over physically demanding tasks like lifting, grocery trips, and home maintenance.

Section D

Wellness Command Center

Mitigate stress, optimize your sleep, and prepare your body to carry the weight of new fatherhood.

🧠
Dad's Mental Health Checklist
1 in 10 expecting dads experience prenatal anxiety or depression. Track your sleep quality, maintain connection with close friends, and speak openly when overwhelmed.
🧘‍♂️
Applied Stress Relief
Practice Box Breathing (inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s) to quickly down-regulate your nervous system during moments of intense pressure.
💪
Concentric Core Strength
Simple functional exercise guidelines: strengthen your back and core (planks, squats, deadlifts) to prepare for carrying baby carriers and car seats safely.
